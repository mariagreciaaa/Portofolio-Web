Little Feet Torto – Digital Marketing Project

A data-driven social media marketing project to increase brand awareness and online sales through TikTok optimization and live commerce.

1. Challenges & Objectives

Little Feet Torto is an MSME specializing in turtle products and care services, facing challenges including low brand awareness, inconsistent social media performance, and untapped potential in online sales. The main objective of this project was to boost organic reach, engagement, and online sales through educational content strategies, TikTok optimization, and live commerce execution.
