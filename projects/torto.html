Little Feet Torto – Digital Marketing Strategy

Digital Marketing Strategist & Content Creator

Little Feet Torto is a small business specializing in turtle products and care services, offering educational content, accessories, and pet care essentials. The brand relies on digital marketing to reach reptile enthusiasts, increase brand awareness, and expand its online sales potential.

1. Tools & Technology Used

TikTok (Main platform for content and performance analytics.) Canva (Designing visual assets and content materials.) CapCut (Editing short-form videos for TikTok.) Google Sheets (Tracking metrics and performance analysis.) Tableau (Data visualization and KPI dashboards.) Google Drive (File storage and project documentation.)
         

2. Key Results

       
  • Total views increased from 1.2K → 2.6M (+38,274%) within 3 months
  • Profile visits grew by 5,068%
  • Followers increased by 43% (from 3,398 to 4,866)
  • Engagement rate improved by 47%
