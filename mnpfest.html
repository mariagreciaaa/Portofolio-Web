MNP Festival 2024 – Digital Marketing & Content Production

A multi-channel digital marketing project focused on content creation, social media optimization, and event promotion.

1. Tantangan dan Tujuan

MNP Festival is an annual campus event involving workshops, competitions, and exhibitions. The main challenge was to grow the event’s online presence from zero and build awareness among students and external participants. The objective of this project was to develop consistent content, increase engagement, and drive audience interest across multiple social media channels.

2. Roles & Tools

Roles:

  • Content Creator
  • Social Media Admin
  • Video & Photo Production
  • Content Planner
  • Event Documentation

Tools:

  • Instagram (feed, reels, promo content)
  • Canva (design & layouting)
  • CapCut (editing reels)
  • Google Drive (file management)
  • Google Sheets (timeline & planning)
